About Lamborghini

Lamborghini was founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. It was created to rival Ferrari. and it quickly became known for producing powerful, design-forward that broke every mold.

Our Vision

To craft machines that are perfect combination of art and power, conuously pushing the boundaries of automative design and performance.

Historical Highlights

  • 1996: Miura - the world's first supercar
  • 2001: Murcielago - a new design marking a new era begins
  • 2023: Revuelto - hybrid technology enters the scene
